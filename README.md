# Middle-Eastern-countries-Sales-Analysis-project
# Business Problem
- The company operates across multiple Middle Eastern countries, but sales performance varies significantly by country, city, payment method, and month.
# Tools Used
- Excel
- Power query
- Power pivot
- Pivot tables
- pivot charts
# Key Insights
- Saudi Arabia is the top-performing country.
- United Arab Emirates and Egypt contribute comparatively lower sales.
- Sales are highly concentrated in key metropolitan areas.
- 24% of sales were not completed.
- Sales steadily increase from January to June.
# Recommendations
- Replicate successful strategies in lower-performing countries.
- Increase marketing investment in Saudi Arabia.
- Optimize checkout and payment processes.
- Implement follow-up reminders for abandoned transactions.
- Increase inventory and marketing spend before peak months (Mayâ€“June).
- Run targeted local marketing campaigns.
- Consider partnerships in weaker areas to increase market penetration.
